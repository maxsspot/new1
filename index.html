A Stop sign indicates that you need to stop. You need to come to a complete stop for around 3 seconds, then you may continue if it is safe.



A Speed Limit sign indicates that maximum speed that you may go.



A Do Not Enter sign indicates a road/area that should not be entered from the way that the sign is facing.



A Wrong Way sign indicates that you are driving the wrong way on the road. It is usually placed after a Do Not Enter sign.



A Yield sign indicates that you should yield to the right of way of oncoming traffic.



A One Way sign indicates that a road can only be driven on one way. The arrow on the sign shows which way.



A No U-Turn sign indicates that you may not make a u-turn.



A No Left-Turn sign indicates that you may not make a left turn.



A Left-Turn Only sign indicates that the lane you are in is specifically for left-turns.



A No Right-Turn sign indicates that you may not make a right turn.



A Right-Turn Only sign indicates that the lane you are in is specifically for right-turns.



A Do Not Pass sign indicates that you may not pass/overtake other vehicles.



A Roundabout sign indicates that there is an upcoming roundabout.



A Pedestrian Crossing sign indicates that there is a crosswalk in line with the sign.



A Traffic-Merging sign indicates that the road is merging from two lanes into one lane.



A Y-Intersection sign indicates that there is an upcoming y-shaped intersection.



A Curve Ahead sign indicates that the road ahead is curving. The arrow can either point left or right.



A No Parking sign indicates that you may not park in a certain area.



A Road Work Ahead sign indicates that there is road work being done on the road ahead.



A No Outlet sign indicates that the road results in a dead end.



A Yield Ahead sign indicates that you will encounter a yield sign in a short distance.



A Stop Ahead sign indicates that you will encounter a stop sign in a short distance.



A Railroad Crossing sign indicates that there is a railroad in a short distance, and you should prepare to take proper safety precautions.



A Pass With Care sign indicates that passing other vehicles is allowed, but you should do it carefully.
